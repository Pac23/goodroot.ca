Raised by the weird, wild

Here for a great time

A Canadian-born Internet native, Goodroot has been contributing to online projects prior to becoming a teenager. Now, fully grown with near two decades of experience, he is as wise as he is hairy.

He first participated in a 'co-foundership' at the age of 12, when he felt that lying about being 16 made him sound old and reputable. Since then, he has engaged in a torrent of upstart tech adventures and expansive intellectual pursuits while exploring mysterious and transformative plant medicines, like Ayahuasca.

He's worked with notable institutions like IBM and the University of British Columbia and has engaged with a mittful of Y-Combinator and Canadian startups. Should you ever want a polite and, erhm, unique perspective, his door is always open.
